Our Story

We're Naman and Aarushi - studying Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology and Informatics at the University of Washington Seattle, and long-time family friends. With extensive experience in app development, product design, and healthtech innovation, we bring a unique blend of technical expertise and compassion-focused engineering to every project we create.

After witnessing the profound isolation many seniors face and the intense stress it places on their caregivers, we combined our passion for meaningful technology with our deep understanding of human-centered design. MySphere represents our commitment to creating solutions that don't just solve problems, but genuinely improve lives.

MySphere is our way of building something that matters - where cutting-edge AI meets heartfelt human connection.
